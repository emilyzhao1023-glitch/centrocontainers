China Shipping Container Parts Supplier

Centro Containers supports international buyers with reliable shipping container parts and dry container parts for maintenance teams, depots, and fabrication workshops. Our supply scope covers container door hardware, ISO corner castings, roof panels, side panels, and other container repair materials used in replacement and overhaul projects. We also provide OEM processing for drawing-based production, including custom size container parts for project-specific dimensions, helping purchasing teams consolidate quality control, lead-time planning, and export coordination through one supplier.
